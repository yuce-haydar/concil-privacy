# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 29, 2026

Council AI Debate ("the App") respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect

The App does not require user registration or account creation.

Conversation history is stored locally on your device using Apple's local storage technologies. We do not store your conversations on our own servers.

## AI Processing

When you submit a prompt, your message is securely sent to third-party AI providers (such as OpenAI or Anthropic) to generate a response. These providers may process your request according to their own privacy policies.

## Data Storage

* No user accounts are created.
* No personal profiles are maintained.
* Conversation history remains on your device unless you delete it.
* We do not maintain a central database containing your conversations.

## Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use advertising networks or cross-app tracking.

## Your Rights

You may delete your conversation history at any time by removing it from within the app or uninstalling the application.

## Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support page provided in the App Store listing.
